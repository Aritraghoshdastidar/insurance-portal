# Realistic Indian Insurance Policies (2025)

## Health Insurance (मेडिक्लेम)

### Individual Health - Basic
- Coverage: ₹2,00,000 - ₹5,00,000
- Annual Premium: ₹8,000 - ₹15,000
- Term: 1-2 years
- Features: Hospitalization, pre-post hospital care, ambulance

### Individual Health - Premium
- Coverage: ₹5,00,000 - ₹10,00,000
- Annual Premium: ₹15,000 - ₹30,000
- Term: 1-3 years
- Features: All basic + day care, critical illness cover

### Family Floater
- Coverage: ₹5,00,000 - ₹25,00,000
- Annual Premium: ₹20,000 - ₹60,000
- Term: 1-2 years
- Features: Covers 4-6 members, maternity, newborn

## Life Insurance (जीवन बीमा)

### Term Life - Basic
- Coverage: ₹10,00,000 - ₹50,00,000
- Annual Premium: ₹6,000 - ₹20,000
- Term: 10-30 years
- Features: Pure protection, no returns

### Term Life - Premium
- Coverage: ₹50,00,000 - ₹2,00,00,000
- Annual Premium: ₹20,000 - ₹80,000
- Term: 10-40 years
- Features: Term + critical illness, accidental death benefit

## Motor Insurance (वाहन बीमा)

### Two Wheeler - Comprehensive
- IDV: ₹40,000 - ₹1,50,000
- Annual Premium: ₹3,500 - ₹8,000
- Term: 1 year
- Features: Own damage + third party + personal accident

### Four Wheeler - Comprehensive
- IDV: ₹3,00,000 - ₹15,00,000
- Annual Premium: ₹12,000 - ₹40,000
- Term: 1-3 years
- Features: Own damage + third party + zero depreciation

## Home Insurance (गृह बीमा)

### Home - Standard
- Coverage: ₹5,00,000 - ₹50,00,000
- Annual Premium: ₹5,000 - ₹25,000
- Term: 1-3 years
- Features: Structure, contents, natural calamities

### Home - Premium
- Coverage: ₹50,00,000 - ₹2,00,00,000
- Annual Premium: ₹25,000 - ₹1,00,000
- Term: 1-5 years
- Features: All standard + valuables, liability

## Travel Insurance (यात्रा बीमा)

### Domestic Travel
- Coverage: ₹1,00,000 - ₹5,00,000
- Premium: ₹200 - ₹1,000 per trip
- Term: Per trip (7-30 days)
- Features: Medical emergency, baggage loss, trip cancellation

### International Travel
- Coverage: ₹5,00,000 - ₹50,00,000
- Premium: ₹1,500 - ₹10,000 per trip
- Term: Per trip (7-180 days)
- Features: All domestic + passport loss, emergency evacuation
